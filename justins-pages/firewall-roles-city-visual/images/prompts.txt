Title: The City Gate — NGFW

Aspect Ratio · 1:1

Purpose: Anchor NGFW as the single fortified point that decides who enters the whole city — the north–south enterprise edge. The viewer should immediately think "this is the one gate between outside and inside, and every arrival is being screened here."

Prompt:
Flat minimalistic but highly detailed vector illustration, storybook-professional style, warm cream off-white background (#faf5e9), no gradients, no photorealism, no glossy 3D. Square 1:1 composition. Wide-angle exterior view of a large, tall, fortified city gate set into a warm sandstone wall — muted-gold (amber) canopy above the gate reading "NGFW · CITY GATE" in soft brown small caps. A single stern but calm uniformed guard in soft brown outline stands at a boom barrier at the centre of the gate, holding up a hand to a caravan of small stylised envelope-shaped travellers waiting outside the wall. Above the guard, a small speech bubble asks "Who is allowed IN?" in soft brown. Behind the wall, we glimpse the busy warm-cream city skyline — rooftops, chimneys, a small stack-of-money-in-an-ice-cube LoFinance emblem on one tower. In the foreground, a clean coral arrow labelled "NORTH ↔ SOUTH" points from the outside world through the gate into the city, making the traffic direction unmistakable. A tiny "FortiGuard" watchtower banner hangs from the wall next to the gate as a subtle Fortinet nod (no real logos). Clean bold soft-brown outlines, coral accents on the arrow and barrier stripes, muted-gold gate canopy, sage-green foliage flanking the gate. Do NOT include: photorealistic faces, real vendor logos other than the described LoFinance ice-cube emblem, dense CLI text, weapons, alarm bells, gradients or glossy 3D.

Accessibility: A large fortified city gate with an amber canopy labelled NGFW · City Gate; a single guard at a boom barrier decides which envelope-shaped travellers may enter, while a coral North↔South arrow shows traffic flowing between the outside world and the city inside.

Memory Anchor: NGFW is the ONE gate at the city wall — the north–south edge decision, "who is allowed IN?"

Title: The Police Inside — ISFW

Aspect Ratio · 1:1

Purpose: Anchor ISFW as the guards that live INSIDE the city and stop lateral movement between neighbourhoods. There is no outside world in view — the threat has already crossed the wall, and this is about containing where it can go next.

Prompt:
Flat minimalistic but highly detailed vector illustration, storybook-professional style, warm cream off-white background (#faf5e9), no gradients, no photorealism, no glossy 3D. Square 1:1 composition. Interior city scene, cross-section perspective, showing two neighbouring city districts separated by a short internal bridge with a small checkpoint hut in the middle. Above the checkpoint hangs a soft-blue canopy reading "ISFW · DISTRICT POLICE" in soft brown small caps. Two friendly but firm police officers in muted-blue uniforms with soft-brown outlines stand at the checkpoint, one politely stopping an envelope-shaped resident from crossing while checking a small card, the other waving a different resident through. On the LEFT district: a "SALES" neighbourhood with small terraced shops and a coral awning; on the RIGHT district: a "FINANCE" neighbourhood with a slightly grander little bank facade and sage-green trees. A tiny stylised malware "mischief goblin" figure (soft coral, small horns, curling smoke tail — cartoonish, not scary) is being turned back at the checkpoint from the Sales side, unable to reach Finance. Above the checkpoint, a small speech bubble reads "Who moves BETWEEN districts?" in soft brown. In the foreground, a clean coral arrow labelled "EAST ↔ WEST" runs horizontally across the bridge to make the lateral direction unmistakable. IMPORTANT: no city wall, no outside gate, and no sky-facing exit visible — the entire scene is inside the city. Clean bold soft-brown outlines, coral accents on arrow and goblin, muted blues on uniforms, sage-green trees. Do NOT include: photorealistic faces, real vendor logos, dense CLI text, weapons, alarm bells, gradients or glossy 3D.

Accessibility: Two inner-city districts (Sales and Finance) linked by a short bridge with a small blue-canopy checkpoint labelled ISFW · District Police; officers stop a small coral malware goblin from crossing from Sales to Finance, while a coral East↔West arrow highlights the lateral direction of the traffic.

Memory Anchor: ISFW is INSIDE the city walls — it stops the goblin from wandering between districts. East–West lateral movement.

Square 1:1 composition. Interior view of a grand bank vault chamber, warm sandstone walls, muted-gold trim. THREE distinct heavy vault doors are drawn side by side across the back of the chamber, each labelled in soft brown small caps: "WEB TIER", "APP TIER", "DB TIER". Between each pair of vault doors stands a calm uniformed vault guard in soft-brown outline and sage-green trim, holding a clipboard and a small stamped access ticket. A short brass trolley track runs between the vault doors carrying small stylised envelope-shaped "requests" from one vault to the next; each request is being checked by the guard before it may pass to the next vault. Above the middle guard hangs a small sage-green canopy reading "DCFW · VAULT SECURITY" in soft brown. A small speech bubble reads "Which vault may talk to which?" in soft brown. On the floor between the vaults, a clean coral arrow labelled "EAST ↔ WEST (server ↔ server)" points sideways across the vault doors to reinforce that the traffic is between servers, not to the outside. Piled inside each open vault: neat stacks of the LoFinance frozen-money ice-cube emblem to signal high-value data. IMPORTANT: no city street, no gate, no sky visible — this is entirely an interior vault scene. Clean bold soft-brown outlines, muted-gold and sage-green accents, coral only on the arrow and access-ticket stamps. Do NOT include: photorealistic faces, real vendor logos other than the LoFinance ice-cube emblem, dense CLI text, weapons, alarm bells, gradients or glossy 3D.

Accessibility: A grand bank vault chamber with three heavy vault doors labelled Web Tier, App Tier and DB Tier; calm guards under a sage-green DCFW · Vault Security canopy check small envelope-shaped requests travelling on a short brass trolley track between the vaults, while a coral East↔West (server↔server) arrow runs across the floor.

Memory Anchor: DCFW lives INSIDE the vault, deciding which tier may talk to which tier. Dense east–west server-to-server, tight rules, high value.

Aspect Ratio · 1:1

Purpose: Anchor DEFW as the SAME small checkpoint replicated at every remote town, all connecting back to the same central city and cloud. The viewer should immediately notice repetition and uniformity across many places — consistency and scale.

Prompt:
Flat minimalistic but highly detailed vector illustration, storybook-professional style, warm cream off-white background (#faf5e9), no gradients, no photorealism, no glossy 3D. Square 1:1 composition. High-angle map-like perspective, gently stylised, showing FIVE small remote towns scattered across a warm cream landscape with sage-green hills and coral farm patches, each town drawn as a tiny cluster of two or three warm-sandstone buildings. In the CENTRE-BACK of the composition sits a slightly larger warm-cream "HQ CITY" cluster with the LoFinance frozen-money ice-cube emblem on its tallest building. In the TOP-RIGHT of the frame, a soft cloud with the small label "CLOUD" hovers above the hills. Each of the five remote towns has an IDENTICAL small checkpoint tower drawn at its edge — same shape, same muted-purple canopy, same soft-brown guard figure standing at the barrier — clearly the same design repeated five times. Above each tower hovers a small ribbon reading "DEFW" in soft brown. Soft glowing coral roads (drawn as clean lines, not gradients) run from every remote-town checkpoint back to HQ and up to the CLOUD, forming a clear hub-and-spoke pattern. A small floating banner in the top-centre reads "CONSISTENT POLICY · EVERY BRANCH" in soft brown small caps. In the foreground, a clean coral arrow labelled "BRANCH ↔ HQ / CLOUD" points from one of the towns toward HQ. IMPORTANT: the visual emphasis must be on REPETITION — the five checkpoints must be clearly identical in shape and colour so the viewer sees uniformity at a glance. Clean bold soft-brown outlines, muted-purple canopies on checkpoints, coral roads and arrow, sage-green landscape. Do NOT include: photorealistic faces, real vendor logos other than the LoFinance ice-cube emblem, dense CLI text, weapons, alarm bells, gradients or glossy 3D.

Accessibility: A stylised map with five small remote towns scattered around a central HQ city and a cloud on the horizon; every town has the same identical muted-purple DEFW checkpoint tower at its edge, all connected back to HQ and cloud by soft coral roads under a banner reading Consistent Policy · Every Branch.

Memory Anchor: DEFW is the SAME checkpoint repeated at every remote town — consistency and scale across distributed branches, all tunnelling back to HQ and cloud.
