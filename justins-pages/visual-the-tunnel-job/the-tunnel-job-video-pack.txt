THE TUNNEL JOB — VIDEO PRODUCTION PACK
Grok video prompts · Narration script · Stitching plan
======================================================

HOW TO USE THIS PACK
--------------------
1. In Grok, use IMAGE-TO-VIDEO for the six scenes you already have
   images for: upload the image, paste the matching VIDEO PROMPT below.
   This preserves your art style and characters exactly.
2. Two scenes have no image (The Job Comes In, The Whisper Room).
   For those, use the TEXT-TO-VIDEO prompts provided — they describe
   the same style so the clips blend in.
3. Generate each clip at 6–10 seconds. If Grok allows "extend", extend
   the Ritual scene — it's the centrepiece and earns 12–15 seconds.
4. Do NOT rely on Grok for voice. Record the narration separately
   (AI TTS or your own voice) and lay it over the stitched cut in a
   video editor (CapCut / DaVinci Resolve — both free). This gives you
   clean timing control.
5. Aspect ratio: 16:9 for everything. The poster is 2:3 — for its clip,
   ask Grok for a slow vertical pan so the portrait fits the frame.

STITCHING PLAN
======================================================
ORDER:      Clip 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 → 6 → 7 → 8
RUNTIME:    ~75–90 seconds total.

TRANSITIONS:
- 1→2: hard cut on a beat of the music (poster ends, story begins).
- 2→3: whip-pan or quick slide left-to-right (same world, new place).
- 3→4: dip to black for half a second — daylight to midnight.
- 4→5: match cut — the grown key turning in the vault lock.
- 5→6: straight cut (same room).
- 6→7: engine-rev sound bridge into the truck rolling.
- 7→8: slow crossfade + music drops to sparse piano (epilogue mood).
- End:  fade to black, then 2s title card: "THE TUNNEL JOB —
        grow it, don't post it." (make in the editor, plain text
        on cream, gold serif.)

MUSIC:
One continuous heist-funk track underneath everything (walking bass,
brass stabs, finger snaps — same brief as the song). Duck the music
-8dB under narration. For Clip 4 (Ritual), if you can, layer a brief
church-organ swell over the funk as the key grows — the tonal clash
is the joke. Kill all music for the final line "Cap the marker,"
leave it dry.

NARRATION VOICE:
One narrator throughout — low, dry, amused, noir-trailer delivery,
slow pace (the lines are written short on purpose; let them
breathe). If your TTS supports it: slight gravel, minimal reverb.
Optional: give "I am Parramatta / Prove it / Sí" tiny character
voice shifts.

SUBTITLE OPTION:
Burn the technical translation as small lower-third captions per
scene (e.g. Clip 3: "IKE_SA_INIT — plaintext by design"). Keeps the
study value even with sound off:
  Clip 2: "interesting traffic triggers IKE"
  Clip 3: "IKE_SA_INIT — proposals · DH publics · nonces · network-id"
  Clip 4: "Diffie-Hellman — grown, not posted"
  Clip 5: "IKE_AUTH — encrypted identities + child SA piggyback"
  Clip 6: "one seed → IKE SA keys + child SA keys"
  Clip 7: "ESP tunnel mode — SPI lookup before decrypt"
  Clip 8: "IKE idle: rekey · DPD · teardown"
