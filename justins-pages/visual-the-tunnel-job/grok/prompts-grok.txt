14 clips · image-to-video · one entry per scene image
=======================================================

HOW TO USE
----------
For each entry: upload the named image to Grok image-to-video,
paste the GLOBAL LINE + that scene's VIDEO PROMPT. Generate 6–10
seconds per clip (scene-05 earns the longest if extension is
available). Record the NARRATION lines separately (TTS or your own
voice) and lay them over the stitched cut in your editor — do not
rely on Grok for voice. Stitch in filename order, 01 → 14.

GLOBAL LINE (paste first, every prompt)
---------------------------------------
Animate this flat vector storybook illustration. Preserve the
exact art style, characters, colours and line work — do not redraw
or restyle. Subtle parallax depth, gentle 2.5D animation,
cinematic heist-movie energy, no photorealism, no added text.
