Pre-Reading — hold this before you paste

A five-minute orientation before each session. Story setup, the mental model to hold, the vocabulary that will appear without ceremony, and 3-4 priming questions that surface the gap the session is going to close.

How to use it. Open the pre-read for the session you're about to run. Skim; don't memorise. Jot rough answers to the orientation questions — this is your before-and-after benchmark. Then head back to the master plan, copy the session prompt, and go.
