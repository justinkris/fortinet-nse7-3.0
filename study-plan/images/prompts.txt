LoFinance — Main Character Portraits
============================================================

STYLE GUIDE (applies to every image below)
- Square 1:1 portrait, head-and-shoulders framing, centered subject.
- Flat minimalistic cartoon vector-art house style. No gradients, no photorealism, no 3D rendering, no anime.
- Warm cream + navy-blue + coral + sage + gold palette (background around #faf5e9 / #f6efe0, accent blues around #1e3a8a / #1e40af).
- Soft, storybook mood. Confident line work. Subtle textured background — plain warm cream, faint paper grain, or the barest hint of a quantitative-trading office (subtle candlestick chart, ledger, ticker tape) that never overpowers the face.
- No text, no captions, no logos on the image itself.
- Save each PNG at the exact filename shown, into: fortinet-nse7-3.0/study-plan/images/
