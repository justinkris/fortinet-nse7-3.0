Flat minimalistic but highly detailed vector illustration, cream off-white background, coral accents, muted gold highlights, sage greens, soft brown outlines, clean bold line work, no gradients, no photorealism, storybook-professional style. Learning purpose: viewer should recall that the NP is stationed at the ports doing repetitive per-packet work including IPsec and header checks, while the CP is the CPU's coprocessor for pattern matching and SSL crypto.
