SESSION 01 · BITE · 5-MINUTE REVISION

Break the Ice — in Five Minutes

Why the perimeter died, why segmentation is forced inspection, and what makes a firewall "next-generation."

1 · THE PERIMETER IS DEAD

A Firewall Only Judges What Crosses It

One checkpoint at the edge creates two zones: outside = hostile, inside = trusted. Modern threats — zero-days, APTs, polymorphic malware, insider threats — start inside via USB sticks, compromised personal devices, phished users. That inside trust is implicit trust, and it enables lateral movement the perimeter never sees. Answer: zero trust — assume compromise, verify everything, everywhere.

🧠
TRIGGER
"No firewall logs at all" = the traffic never crossed an inspection point. Path problem, not feature problem.
2 · SEGMENTATION & BLAST RADIUS

Compartments Like a Ship

The goal shifts from prevent entry to contain damage. Segmentation is only real when inter-segment traffic is forced through an inspection point — gateway on the FortiGate, not the switch. A VLAN gatewayed on a switch is a filing cabinet with labelled but unlocked drawers. One flooded compartment doesn't sink the vessel; controls scale with asset sensitivity (servers first), and every inspection layer costs latency — at LoFinance, milliseconds are money.

3 · NGFW & UTM

Envelope vs Letter

Port/IP filtering reads headers — envelopes. With cricket streams, banking, and malware C2 all wearing 443, envelopes are worthless. The NGFW is a superset: it keeps ACLs, port/IP filtering, and stateful inspection, and adds application-layer analysis + UTM engines:

EngineInspects
AntivirusFiles (the oldest engine — don't forget it)
IPSTraffic — exploits & anomalies
Web filteringDestinations by category/reputation
Application controlThe app itself, regardless of port
Advanced threat protectionThe unknown — sandboxing
SSL inspectionEnabler — decrypts so the others can see
Exam: AV inspects files, IPS inspects traffic. "Replaces" is the poison word — NGFW incorporates and adds.
4 · THE FORTINET SOLUTION

One OS, One Console, One Immune System

  • Spectrum: FortiGate 30-90 (branch) · 100-900 (campus) · 1000-7000 (data center) · VM/FortiCNF/FortiSASE (cloud).
  • FortiOS everywhere; FortiManager as the single unified management console.
  • FortiGuard Labs: AI/ML threat intelligence pushed globally in near real time. Config ≠ protection — engines are only as good as their feed.
  • Inspection runs north/south (in/out) and east/west (between internal segments).

Role names for the boxes — NGFW / ISFW / DCFW / DEFW — are Session 2.
