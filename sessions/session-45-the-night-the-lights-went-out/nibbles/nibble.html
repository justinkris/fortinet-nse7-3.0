The soul

Shared brain: config sync + session sync + one vMAC/IP identity. A-P = one works, one waits. A-A = primary receives everything first, load-balances at the price of an inter-chassis hop.

Formation blockers

Model · Firmware · Hard-drive config · Operating mode. Licensing never blocks — cluster runs at the poorest member's level.

Election (default)

Monitored links → uptime (≥5 min gap) → priority → serial. Facts about now beat preferences from Tuesday. Override (CLI only) lifts priority above uptime — and bills you a second failover on recovery.

Graceful failover

Default: diagnose sys ha reset-uptime on the primary. Override on: change priorities. Override & priority never sync between members.

The pulse

0x8890/0x8891 = alive. 0x8893 = identical. Two heartbeat links or risk split-brain: two survivors, one identity, chaos.
