● VISUAL LEARNING · SESSION 29

Two Layers of Truth

A config's journey between two databases — and the box caught in the middle.

Recommended visual style — "Config Journey + Two Truths" This session is about two stored copies of a config disagreeing and truth travelling in a chosen direction. The strongest memory anchors are (1) a split "two-layer" diagram of one box's config, (2) a directional flow of Install-down vs Retrieve-up, and (3) the emotional "haunting" scene. Below are five illustrated placeholders in the LoFinance house style — flat vector, cream background, coral/blue/sage palette, soft brown outlines, no gradients or photorealism.
