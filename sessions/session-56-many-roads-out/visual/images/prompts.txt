Title: Two Roads Out of LoFinance

Prompt:
Flat minimalistic but highly detailed illustrated vector artwork. Cream / warm off-white background (#faf5e9). Coral accents, muted gold highlights, sage greens, soft brown outlines, deep navy for structure. Clean bold line work. No gradients, no photorealism, no glossy 3D rendering. Generous spacing. Professional but storybook-like, clear enough to function as an educational diagram. Recurring cast appearance must stay consistent: SEÑOR is a compact older man in a broad straw sombrero, rumpled cardigan, always near a whiteboard, calm and slightly eccentric, often holding a peeled mandarin. THE INTERMEDIATE ENGINEER is a competent adult in a plain shirt with rolled sleeves, no hat, drawn as the person actually solving things. JUNIOR is a young man, youthful overconfidence, oversized headphones around his neck. PENNY is a poised woman in business attire holding a folder. RILEY wears a help-desk headset. J.R. is a quiet figure with a small notebook and a clicky pen. The LoFinance logo is stacks of banknotes frozen inside a pale blue ice cube. Composition: a wide illustrated aerial-perspective scene. On the left, a stylised office building marked with the LoFinance logo (banknotes frozen in a pale blue ice cube) on its facade. Two roads leave the building heading right toward two separate cloud-shaped destinations labelled 'ISP-1 PARRAMATTA' and 'ISP-2 CHATSWOOD'. The upper road is busy: a dense, cheerful stream of small stylised delivery vans nose-to-tail, coral coloured, flowing continuously. The lower road is immaculate, freshly painted, completely empty — not a single vehicle — with a small toll booth beside it showing a paper invoice with a visible dollar amount, drawn in muted gold. A boom gate on the empty road is DOWN, and the gate is labelled with a tiny tag reading 'best path only'. Foreground lower-left: PENNY stands with her folder, arm extended toward the empty road, expression composed and unimpressed. Beside her the INTERMEDIATE ENGINEER looks along the empty road thoughtfully. Small in the mid-ground: SEÑOR leans against a signpost holding a peeled mandarin, watching rather than intervening. Signpost text reads 'TWO ROADS'. A small chalk-style caption at the bottom of the frame reads: 'Learned plurally. Installed singularly.' Colour hierarchy: the busy road in coral, the idle road in sage green with gold invoice accents, buildings in deep navy line work on cream. Must NOT appear: any router icons, any CLI text, any network cables, any technical labels beyond the two ISP cloud names and the two captions, no BAZ character in this panel.
