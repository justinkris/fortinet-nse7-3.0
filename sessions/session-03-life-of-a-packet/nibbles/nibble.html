Session 03 · 1-Minute Nibble

Life of a Packet

Order of opsCheap checks (NP: ACL/HPE/header) → expensive checks (CP: IPS/AV/SSL) last.
NP vs CPNP = forwarding, wire-speed, interface-level. CP = content, pattern matching / decrypt.
Pipeline orderDNAT → Routing → Policy Lookup → SNAT.
Never a no-opPolicy lookup / implicit deny — applies to every packet, everywhere.
Exam trapFIN/RST → always CPU. Handshake ≠ "still" happening on an established session.
