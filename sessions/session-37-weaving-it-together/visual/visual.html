The Topology Reveal

Recommended visual style: The Detective's Enterprise Map. This session is a discovery story — a network becoming visible, then a hidden intruder surfacing on the map. The strongest memory encoding combines an enterprise topology reveal (the fabric blooming) with a detective-board investigation (Minerva), plus one "reading the room" character piece for device detection. Four illustrations, one narrative thread: light spreading across a dark map until the thing hiding in it has nowhere left to stand.
