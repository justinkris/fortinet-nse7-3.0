The Parramatta Project

The rule: pick the topology whose weakness lands where you have no traffic. LoFinance's feed lives at HQ → hub-and-spoke.
Mesh: n(n−1)/2 tunnels — 10 sites = 45. Hub-and-spoke: n−1 — 10 sites = 9.
Hub tax: spoke↔spoke = double WAN latency + hub must decrypt/re-encrypt (separate keys per tunnel). Hub = bottleneck + SPOF.
ipsec-aggregate = LACP for tunnels. redundant = active/backup · L3/L4 = per-flow · round-robin = per-packet.
Trap: round-robin + unequal latency = out-of-order packets = glitchy feed.
Selectors: one phase 1, many phase 2s — "a trunk link, but over a tunnel." No selector match → never enters the tunnel. Declared pipe, not an open hose.
Trap: "spokes share tunnel keys" — FALSE, always. Own SA, own keys, per tunnel.
