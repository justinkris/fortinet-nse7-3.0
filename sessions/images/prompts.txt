Sessions Hub — Card Background Images
====================================================================

House style (applies to every prompt below):
- Flat minimalistic cartoon vector art. No gradients, no photorealism,
  no 3D, no lens flares, no gloss.
- Warm palette: cream (#faf5e9), soft coral, sage green, muted gold,
  and a single deep-blue accent (#1e40af) to echo the site theme.
- Storybook mood — playful, calm, slightly nostalgic. Composition
  should read at thumbnail size (square, ~600×600).
- Leave the LOWER THIRD of the image intentionally light / soft so
  a scrim can fade to cream and the session number pill + title
  stays legible on top. Do not place important detail in the lower
  ~35% of the frame.
- No text or type on the image. No logos.
- Single-scene composition (not a comic grid). The metaphor should
  be legible in one glance.

====================================================================

session-01-break-the-ice.png

Title: Session 01 — Break the Ice

A tiny explorer in a scarf and mittens standing at the edge of a
frozen pond, gently tapping the ice with a mallet. A hairline crack
spreads outward in a friendly geometric pattern. Behind them, a
warm cabin glows softly in the distance, smoke curling from the
chimney. Snowflakes drift down in flat cartoon shapes. Cream sky,
sage pines on the horizon, coral scarf. The overall mood is
"first day of school for firewalls" — inviting, low-stakes, the
beginning of a journey.

session-02-where-the-walls-go.png

Title: Session 02 — Where the Walls Go

A top-down / isometric flat cartoon view of a small storybook
village. A friendly architect in a gold hard-hat is placing tall
brick walls at the choke points between neighborhoods — one wall
between the market square and the outskirts, one between the
harbor and the town center. Little roads (blue) thread through
the gaps, and tiny travelers walk along them. The walls are the
same warm brick with a subtle blue banner on top. Show intent:
walls at the boundaries, never inside a neighborhood. Sage trees
dot the edges. Convey "placement decisions matter."

session-03-life-of-a-packet.png

Title: Session 03 — Life of a Packet

A cheerful anthropomorphic envelope (the packet) walking along a
winding path through a series of tidy checkpoint booths. Each
booth has a different colored pennant — one sage, one coral, one
gold, one deep-blue — representing different inspection stages
(ingress, policy, NAT, egress). A friendly clerk peeks out of
one booth stamping the envelope's passport. The path curves
gently from left (arrival) toward the horizon (delivery), where
a small mailbox waits. Warm cream sky. Convey "a journey with
stops," not surveillance.

session-04-the-floor-wars.png

Title: Session 04 — The Floor Wars

A whimsical open-plan office / warehouse floor split into
territories by low painted lines on the ground. Two rival teams
(distinguished only by coral vs. sage badges) stand near their
edges, holding clipboards, negotiating over which team's rules
apply to shared aisles. A neutral referee in a gold vest stands
at the intersection holding up a small rulebook. The lines on
the floor form crisp rectangles like policy zones. No violence,
no anger — the "war" is polite bureaucratic turf-marking.
Convey "ordered rules on a shared floor."

session-05-invisible-fences.png

Title: Session 05 — Invisible Fences

A rolling meadow in flat cartoon style with a few friendly farm
animals (a sheep, a rabbit, a fox) wandering. Faint dashed lines
in soft blue trace invisible boundaries across the field —
shimmering just barely, like a heat haze. The fox pauses at one
of the invisible lines, one paw lifted, understanding it cannot
cross. A shepherd on a distant hill holds a small remote with a
gold antenna. Warm cream sky, sage grass, coral wildflowers.
Convey "rules you can't see, but they still hold."

session-06-one-box-many-firewalls.png

Title: Session 06 — One Box, Many Firewalls

A single large storybook cottage (the FortiGate box) with a
cross-section cutaway view revealing three or four completely
separate little apartments inside — each with its own front
door, its own tiny occupant (a mouse, a rabbit, a bird, a
squirrel), its own furniture, its own color scheme (sage,
coral, gold, blue). The apartments share the outer walls of
the cottage but never see each other. On the roof, a single
chimney with a small blue banner. Convey "one shell, many
independent tenants" — the core VDOM idea.

session-07-bridges-between-worlds.png

Title: Session 07 — Bridges Between Worlds

Two small storybook islands floating gently in a cream sky,
each a different palette (one sage-forested, one coral-roofed
harbor town). A slender wooden footbridge with soft blue
railings arches between them. A tiny courier in a gold cap
walks across the bridge carrying a letter. Below the bridge,
faint dotted lines show the "route" being taken. In the far
distance, a third island hints at more networks beyond.
Convey "controlled crossings between separate worlds" —
inter-VDOM links, transparent-mode bridges, the idea of
deliberate passage.
