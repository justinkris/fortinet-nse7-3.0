Where the Walls Go

Three addresses, one job description. Edge fights content; interior fights lateral movement; the DC fights physics.
DEFWBranch · 30–90 · ≤1 Gbps · IPsec home, no deep analysis
ISFWCampus · 100–900 · 1–100 Gbps · micro-segmentation, low latency, W/LAN
DCFWData centre · 1000–7000 · 10–1000 Gbps · hyperscale, LAG to switches
NGFWA ROLE · 100–7000 + VM/cloud · 1–40 Gbps · multilayered security
The trap: role ≠ model. ISFW + UTM profiles = it's now an NGFW. Same box, same invoice. East-west dwarfs north-south. Backhauled branch → protection lives at HQ. "We pay for placement, not duplication."
